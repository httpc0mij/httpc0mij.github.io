HP Printer Setup with Windows

  1. Connect the printer and the windows via the USB Cable.
  2. Navigate to the settings from the start option on the windows.
  3. Tap on the devices and choose the printers or scanners option.
  4. Then click on the add a printer or scanner option.
  5. Wait until your printer’s name is visible on the windows.
  6. Then after getting your printer’s name tap on the name.
  7. The process to add the printer to the windows.

HP Printer Setup with MAC

  1. Tap on the Apple icon on the top.
  2. Then choose the system preference option.
  3. Click the printers and scanners option.
  4. Add the printer to the printer by tapping on the +icon on the MAC device.
  5. Then you need to enter the IP Address of the printer.
  6. Then tap on the add option found on the down corner to add the printer to the MAC device.
  7. Now we follow the given process of HP Printer setup for Windows and Mac via 123.hp.com/setup.
